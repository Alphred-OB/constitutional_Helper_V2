Architecture & Flow

How it Works.

Demystifying the technology behind Ghana's smartest constitutional AI assistant.

01 The Knowledge Base

1

Semantic Chunking

Instead of random cuts, we slice the law into "Meaningful Chapters." It’s like dividing a book by its stories rather than just page numbers. This keeps the legal context whole.

2

Digital Fingerprints

We use Vectorization to turn words into a "Digital Fingerprint." The computer sees a unique code that represents the meaning of the law, not just the text.

3

384-D Maps

Think of a normal map (2D). Our "Map of Law" has 384 directions. This allows the system to find an idea (like Liberty) even if you ask about it using different words.

02 The User Journey

Land & Explore

User arrives at the Helper and sees the "Popular Inquiries" cards—shortcuts to common constitutional questions.

Ask Anything

User types a question in English or Twi. The system instantly begins its semantic search across the 1992 Constitution.

Real-Time Context

The system finds the Top 5 most relevant articles. It "reads" them in milliseconds to ensure the answer is grounded in facts.

Verified Response

User receives a direct answer with exact references. They can listen to the response or read the original articles to verify.

03 Behind the Scenes

User Question
The user types a question in English or Twi into the chat interface.
Meaning Matcher (Semantic Search)
The system converts your question into a "Digital Fingerprint" and scans the entire constitution for matches in milliseconds.
Legal Context Building
The top 5 matching articles are pulled from the database and formatted into a "brief" for the AI to study.
AI Intelligence (Llama 3.1)
The AI reads only the selected articles to ensure the final answer is 100% grounded in the 1992 Constitution.
Human-Friendly Answer
The final response is delivered with citations and optional audio for accessibility.

04 Staying "Inside the Box"

Safety & Truth

To prevent the AI from making things up, we’ve locked it inside a strict "Knowledge Box."

Zero-Knowledge Rule

If a fact isn't in the 1992 Constitution, the AI won't guess. It is forbidden from using outside information.

Relevance Lock

If you ask about football or food, the system sees it doesn't match the law and tells you it can only help with the Constitution.

No-Slop Policy

We've stripped away the "AI-talk" (like "Certainly!" or "I hope this helps"). You get straight, professional answers.

Strict Identity

The AI identifies only as a Ghana Constitutional Expert. It will refuse to talk about laws from other countries or act as anything other than your legal guide.
