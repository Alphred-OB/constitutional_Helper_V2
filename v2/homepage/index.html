Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free?
Yes, Constitutional Helper is completely free to use. No registration, no subscription fees, no hidden costs. Everyone in Ghana can access it for free.
Do I need internet?
Yes. Constitutional Guide runs online in your browser, so you need internet access to use it.
How accurate are the answers?
Every answer is grounded in Ghana's 1992 Constitution with direct citations. The AI searches through constitutional articles and provides answers backed by official text. We continuously improve accuracy based on user feedback.
What if it gives a wrong answer?
If you encounter an inaccurate answer, please report it through our GitHub repository. Your feedback helps us improve the system continuously.
What languages are supported?
Constitutional Helper currently supports English and Asante Twi. You can ask questions in either language and receive responses in your preferred language.
Do I need to install anything?
No. You can open the app directly online with no installation.
